Event Structure

Feature Details
Total Episodes 4 biweekly episodes + 1 Finale

Mission: Provide aspiring entrepreneurs with exposure, idea validation, and confidence-building in their entrepreneurial journey.

Vision: To identify and pursue one validated idea annually by forming a team and developing it into a tangible business.

  • Opportunity to expand network & form teams
  • Potential funding & mentorship from industry leaders
  • The winning idea will be actively developed over the next year
